ALSAAD PHARMACY

Alsaad Pharmacy stands as one of the leading pharmacies in Peshawar, offering comprehensive retail services to the public. Our diverse range includes pharmaceuticals, nutraceuticals, cosmetics, and toiletries. Additionally, we provide essential community pharmacy services, offering over-the-counter medications, patient education, counseling, and first aid through a team of qualified and experienced professionals, including pharmacists, pharmacy technicians, and health technicians. Umar Pharmacy is dedicated to supporting the professional growth of fresh pharmacy graduates and business administration graduates by providing internship and training opportunities in the retail and community pharmacy sectors. Our mission is to deliver a complete range of pharmaceutical, herbal, nutraceutical, surgical, and cosmetic products at reasonable rates, ensuring high quality and authenticity.
